GREAT TO CONNECT WITH YOU

I'M SHRAVANI

Welcome to Arka Healings! I’m Shravani – a yoga teacher, sound healer, and passionate advocate for holistic well-being. My journey began with a love for the arts and a deep curiosity for ancient healing practices. With a BCA degree and nearly a decade in the corporate world, I discovered the importance of balance and transitioned into wellness to guide others toward a more mindful life.


Since 2016, I’ve been teaching therapeutic yoga, holding a diploma from Svyasa University, Bangalore. I’ve led both group and personal sessions, including corporate workshops for International Yoga Day. My work focuses on nurturing the body, mind, and spirit through intentional practice.


My journey into sound healing took root in Rishikesh, where I trained and mentored over 100 sound healers globally. Blending traditional and modern techniques, I offer transformative sessions that create a space for inner harmony. At Arka Healings, I weave my love for dance, painting, and culture into a holistic path for well-being.

My journey includes teaching yoga at naturopathy centers, where I collaborated with healthcare professionals to deliver yoga as a core part of holistic healing programs. This experience has deepened my understanding of how yoga can complement natural therapies, offering relief and support for a range of health concerns.



"With each breath, the noise within softens. With each sound, the heart remembers its natural rhythm."



Through customized therapeutic yoga sessions, I aim to empower individuals to restore balance, enhance vitality, and cultivate long-term wellness. Whether you seek to manage a specific health condition or embrace a preventive lifestyle, my approach is tailored to meet your unique needs.
