SHRAVANI

Welcome to Arka Healings! I am a passionate yoga teacher, sound healer, and advocate for holistic well-being. My journey into the world of healing and wellness is rooted in a lifelong love for the arts, a curiosity for holistic practices, and a desire to guide others toward balance and harmony.


Born and raised in, Karnataka, India I completed my schooling in Chinthamani and later earned a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Applications (BCA) from Bangalore.


For nearly a decade, I worked in the corporate sector, where I experienced firsthand the importance of maintaining physical and mental well-being in a fast-paced world. This realization led me to explore ancient practices that nurture the body, mind, and spirit.

"I’ve trained extensively in Rishikesh, where I’ve had the honor of mentoring over 100 sound healers who now share their healing practices worldwide"

My approach seamlessly blends traditional healing techniques with modern sound healing methods, creating transformative experiences that foster deep inner harmony. Sound has been used for centuries as a powerful tool for healing, and through carefully curated sessions and workshops, I harness its therapeutic potential to guide individuals toward balance and self-discovery.Each session is designed to be a safe and nurturing space where participants can release stress, restore emotional equilibrium, and reconnect with their inner selves.



Beyond my healing practice, my artistic journey has been equally influential in shaping my holistic approach. Having trained in classical dance for over three years, I have developed a deep appreciation for movement as a form of expression and healing.


At Arka, I bring together these diverse experiences to provide a comprehensive approach to well-being. My offerings extend beyond yoga and sound healing to specialized workshops that empower individuals to cultivate inner peace, resilience, and overall wellness.

Join me on this journey – where ancient wisdom meets modern healing.
